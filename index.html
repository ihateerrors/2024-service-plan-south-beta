2024 Service Plan Proposal – South Subarea

South Subarea – ST Express Bus Service

In the summer of 2021, Sound Transit gathered feedback from the community on a number of service improvements across the transit system. These improvements were adopted by the Sound Transit Board and planned for implementation during 2022. Due to ongoing workforce challenges since 2022, Sound Transit has not been able to fully implement many of those service improvements including more frequent service and the addition of evening or weekend service on several routes.

Sound Transit is committed to improving service, but the agency will need to adjust and prioritize as we continue to see shortages of bus operators and maintainers. We are seeking feedback from the public on some proposed solutions to improve service within the current operational constraints. This May, Sound Transit consulted with [placeholder for May engagement feedback and data].

Help us shape the future of the Sound Transit Express Network. Your feedback on proposals to adjust service levels, stop locations or routing changes for select ST Express routes will help us determine service plans.

ST Express changes

Given the challenges filling both bus operator and maintainer positions, as well as spare parts supply chain issues, Sound Transit does not anticipate growth in ST Express service in 2024. Given these contraints, we must continue to defer the full implementation of the Board adopted 2022 and 2023 service plans. These plans prioritized improved service equity in south King County and Pierce County. However, Sound Transit is committed to finding interim solutions to improve service in the South Subarea. The following Express bus route changes were identified with the help of community feedback during [placeholder for May engagement effort].

To see how your Express bus route may change, see the proposed changes below.
